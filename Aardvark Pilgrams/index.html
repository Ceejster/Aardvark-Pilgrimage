The Journey

We started in Southern China, in the famous city of "eternal spring", Kunming. This is one of the last (and highest) major cities until hitting Vietnam. We met while playing rugby togehter and decided that, before leaving China, and Asia, a cycling pilgrimage across borders was needed. Many gooches were raw and sore to even look at but not saddeling never crossed our minds. Day in and day out, the four of us saddled up and completed a 12 day, just over 1,000km cycle from Kunming to Vietnams stunning, and bustling, capital city Hanoi. Regarless of what people thought.
